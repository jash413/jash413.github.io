Our story

Passionate cat lovers and experts in conservation, wildlife biology, and data analysis came together with a mission to raise awareness and promote conservation efforts for endangered cat species. They created the "Catstats" website to provide a reliable source of information on the population, habitat, and conservation status of various species of wild cats, including tigers, lions, leopards, and cheetahs. The team worked tirelessly to design a user-friendly website that would be accessible to anyone who wanted to learn more about these amazing animals and the importance of protecting them. The website quickly became a valuable resource for people all over the world, inspiring many to take action to protect these incredible animals from extinction.

We are a team of dedicated cat lovers who believe in the importance of conserving the world's feline species. Our team consists of wildlife experts, conservationists, and researchers who have a passion for cats and their natural habitats. We are committed to using our knowledge and expertise to make a difference in the world and help preserve these magnificent animals for generations to come.

At the "Catstats" our primary mission is to provide information on endangered cat species around the world. We have collected data on the population, habitat, and conservation status of various species, including tigers, lions, leopards, cheetahs, and more. We collaborate with wildlife organizations and experts to ensure that our data is accurate and up-to-date. Our website is a valuable resource for individuals and organizations that want to learn more about these animals and the importance of protecting them.

We are passionate about cats and their role in the world's ecosystems. These animals are essential to maintaining the delicate balance of nature, and their extinction would have significant implications for the environment and other species. We created the "Catstats" to raise awareness about the threats facing these animals and to encourage action to protect them. By providing information on endangered cat species, we hope to inspire individuals and organizations to take action and make a difference in the world. Together, we can work towards a future where cats thrive in their natural habitats and continue to play a vital role in the world's ecosystems.
